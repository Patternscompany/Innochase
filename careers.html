Current Openings

AI / ML Engineer

Experience: 2-5 years in AI/ML development

Requirements: Proficiency in Python, TensorFlow/PyTorch, scikit-learn, and data analysis. Experience with cloud platforms (AWS/Azure) and version control (Git).

Responsibilities: Develop and deploy machine learning models, collaborate with data scientists, optimize algorithms for performance, and contribute to AI-driven solutions.

Cyber Security Analyst

Experience: 3-6 years in cybersecurity

Requirements: Knowledge of network security, firewalls, intrusion detection systems, and compliance standards (ISO 27001, NIST). Certifications like CISSP or CEH preferred.

Responsibilities: Monitor security threats, conduct vulnerability assessments, implement security measures, and respond to incidents to protect organizational assets.

Frontend Developer

Experience: 2-4 years in frontend development

Requirements: Strong skills in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React.js, and responsive design. Experience with version control and UI/UX principles.

Responsibilities: Build user-friendly interfaces, optimize web performance, collaborate with designers, and ensure cross-browser compatibility.

Digital Marketing Specialist

Experience: 2-5 years in digital marketing

Requirements: Expertise in SEO, SEM, social media marketing, Google Analytics, and content creation. Experience with marketing automation tools.

Responsibilities: Develop marketing strategies, manage campaigns, analyze performance metrics, and drive online growth for clients.
